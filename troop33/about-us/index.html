About Us

Who are we?

We are a local Boy Scout troop in Staunton sponsored by the St Francis of Assisi Catholic Church. Our goal is to help the young adults of Staunton to grow into better people by learning valuable skills while having fun along the way. We also emphasize service to the community by doing projects with the betterment of the community in mind.

What do we do?

Our activities are mostly determined by the boy leadership within the troop. We aim to have one major activity each month with weekly meetings. Our big monthly activities include camping, hiking, backpacking, and various other fun activities determined by our scouts. Our weekly meetings are held from 7 to 8:30 pm every Wednesday at St Francis of Assisi Catholic Church located at 118 N New Street.
